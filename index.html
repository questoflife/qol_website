Peer Coaching and Community for Gamers Worldwide

Life is supposed to be a Co-Op game. Our groups provide a safe and supportive space in whom you'll get to know yourself better, level up your communication skills, build meaningful connections with others in your group, and help each other get unstuck and move towards your goals.

With People Who Care

We're a not-for-profit community interest company powered by donations.

Our groups are run by an amazing team of volunteers, many of which started as members in our groups and now want to give back to the community.

Anywhere in the World

We meet online on Discord and have members from 30+ countries.

If you've completed at least one of our 16 week groups and want to give back to the community, consider applying to join our team of volunteer facilitators.

You will receive training where you're learn theory about topics such as coaching, the mind, and ethics. Training also has a huge hands on component consisting of group discussions, roleplay, and both supervised and unsupervised 1-1 coaching practice.

After completing training you'll get access to our facilitator server where we run weekly workshops, office hours, 1-1 support for coaching-related or personal struggles, group support sessions and more.
