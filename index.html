What to Expect

Join a group of 4–6 other members

Meet once a week for 4-16 weeks

Receive coaching support in a facilitated and inclusive environment

Practice communication skills

Support others in your group

On the other side of Quest of Life, you'll have a safe space in which to explore your emotions, be listened to. As you learn to support each other effectively, you'll level up your communication skills, learn to connect with others, build meaningful relationships, feel like you're part of a group and develop a sense of belonging.
