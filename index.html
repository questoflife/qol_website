What to Expect

Join a group of 4–6 other members

Meet once a week for 4-16 weeks

Receive coaching support in a facilitated and inclusive environment

Practice communication skills

Support others in your group

On the other side of Quest of Life, you'll have a safe space in which to explore your emotions, be listened to. As you learn to support each other effectively, you'll level up your communication skills, learn to connect with others, build meaningful relationships, feel like you're part of a group and develop a sense of belonging.

About Us

Quest of Life is led by a diverse team of volunteers passionate about creating spaces for connection, growth, and mutual support.

Our mission is to foster personal growth, mutual support, and meaningful connections among young adults navigating life’s challenges.

Your donations help us provide free and accessible peer coaching to young adults worldwide, enabling us to build confidence and connection.

We believe in transparency—check out our Donation Impact Spreadsheet to see exactly how your contributions are making a difference.
