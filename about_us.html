Who We Are

We're a not-for-profit Community Interest Company offering online peer support to people from all over the world.

Our Mission

We offer free support to anyone who needs it, especially those who otherwise can't access or afford coaching or therapy.

What We Do

We run free peer support groups on Discord, facilitated by an awesome group of passionate and caring volunteers, many of which initially participated in our groups as members.
