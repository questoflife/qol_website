About Us

Life is meant to be played co-op.

At Quest of Life we believe that everyone should have access to a supportive space where they can feel safe, heard and understood. A space in which nobody has to feel alone. We're on a mission to create this kind of support for anyone who needs it, and especially those who can't access it elsewhere.

Growing up, many of us struggled with social skills, making friends, fitting in, feeling like the weird kid, feeling alone... But if we've learned one thing, it's that no matter what you're going through, or how broken you might feel, it's not just you. And life gets a hell of a lot easier if you have a team rooting for you. Maybe we can help you find yours.

We're a passionate team of volunteers here to help our peers as best as we can. Several of us started out as members in one of our groups, and, after seeing our lives change for the better, decided we wanted to give back to the community and become facilitators.
