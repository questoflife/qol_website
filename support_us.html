Coaching and Facilitation Training

We're always on the lookout for passionate and caring volunteers who want to give back to the community by running groups or offering 1-on-1 coaching. Please note that being accepted into traing doesn't guarantee progression to becoming a Quest of Life facilitator.

Responsibilities

  • Facilitate peer support groups either by yourself or with a co-facilitator and/or offer 1-on-1 coaching
  • Proactively reach out for support with any issues you encounter in your work with clients
  • Complete post-session documentation
  • Work in alignment with our Ethical Framework

Requirements

  • You want to give back to the community by becoming a Quest of Life facilitator
  • You've completed at least one of our groups. 16 week groups are preferable, but we will accept applicants who've only completed a 4 week group.
  • You're curious and eager to learn more about coaching and group facilitation
  • You're ready to go out your comfort zone and want to be challenged during training
  • You're aligned with Quest of Life's values
  • You're a caring person and empathetic listener - you don't feel the need to "fix" others
  • You're consistent, and able to work independently - managing your own schedule and making sure to fulfil your responsibilities with minimal oversight
  • You're driven to give back to the community and want to create a positive impact
  • You're a strong communicator in both written and verbal form
  • You're able to stay remain calm in any situation
  • You're friendly and approachable - easy to talk to and easy to trust and open up to
  • You value difference and are highly accepting of individuals from all backgrounds
  • You already have a strong support system.

Benefits (training)

  • Free coaching/facilitation training developed and run by current Quest of Life facilitators
  • On successful completion of training, you may be invited to become a Quest of Life facilitator
  • On completion of training, you will get access to our social server for Quest of Life volunteers past and present

Benefits (for active facilitators)

  • Join a community of passionate and caring individuals
  • Access to ongoing learning opportunities such as workshops in our facilitator server
  • Access to coaching and facilitation related support in group Case Review sessions and during experienced facilitators' Office Hours
  • Access to general 1-1 support for anything work or life related
  • Opportunities to contribute towards shaping the direction of the organisation

Community Manager

We're looking for a community manager to help us grow and maintain our Discord community. If you've been active in our community and enjoy interacting with others, you might be a great fit for the role! Currently our main goal is updating the community on all the exciting things happening behind the scenes, but we also think the server could be much more than just a booking portal for groups. We're looking for someone who is proactive and driven and creative, and wants to work with us and the community to figure out what we can offer beyond running groups, and how to put it into action.

Responsibilities

  • Work with the mod team to maintain a positive and welcoming atmosphere, promptly resolve conflicts, and answer community questions.
  • Communicate organizational updates and other announcements to the community
  • Shape the direction of what Quest of Life can offer on Discord beyond groups and coaching
  • Plan and execute engaging long-term and short-term content plans tailored to the needs of the community
  • Foster interactions and increase engagement on the server through creative initiatives

Requirements

  • Be an active participant in the community
  • Be an empathetic listener with strong communication skills, both written and spoken
  • Ability to plan and execute tasks with minimal supervision

Benefits

  • Shape the direction of Quest of Life
  • Fulfilling work with a lot of autonomy
  • Access to support spaces and 1-1 support for volunteers
  • Access to our social server on Discord for Quest of Life volunteers

Discord Moderator

As our Discord server grows, we anticipate more activity and an increasing need for moderation. We're therefore on the lookout for volunteers who care about helping us foster a safe and welcoming space for our community.

Responsibilities

  • You're a caring and compassionate person
  • You value difference and are highly accepting of people from any background
  • You're able to work independently with minimal supervision
  • You have incredibly strong communication skills in both written and verbal form
  • You're highly skilled at conflict resolution
  • You're able to remain impartial, calm and collected under any circumstances
  • You're comfortable reaching out for support when you need it

Requirements

  • Promptly respond to incidents in the server and document them according to protocol
  • Foster a safe and welcoming atmosphere within the server
  • Proactively raise issues to the head moderator if you're unsure how to handle a particular situation or if you're questioning a decision you made

Benefits

  • Access to our social server on Discord for Quest of Life volunteers
  • Access to moderation-related and personal support
