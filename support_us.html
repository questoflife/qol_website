Quest of Life is led by a diverse team of volunteers passionate about creating spaces for connection, growth, and mutual support.

Our mission is to foster personal growth, mutual support, and meaningful connections among young adults navigating life’s challenges.

Your donations help us provide free and accessible peer coaching to young adults worldwide, enabling us to build confidence and connection.




Currently we have donation links set up in three currencies - £ GBP, $ USD and € EUR. For any other currency or for custom amounts, please email us at admin@questoflife.org stating the amount, your preferred currency, and whether you'd like to make a one-off or a recurring donation. We will then set up a custom donation link for you.

Coaching and Facilitation Training

We're always on the lookout for passionate and caring volunteers who want to give back to the community by running groups or offering 1-on-1 coaching. Please note that being accepted into training doesn't guarantee progression to becoming a Quest of Life facilitator.

What to Expect from Training

  • Training will consist of two 8 week blocks with an assessment at the end of each block that will determine whether you can continue to the next stage
  • You will learn about topics including Motivational Interviewing, ethics, stages of change, the vedic model of the mind, motivation and goal setting, practical coaching skills, compassion fatigue
  • Weekly group sessions (2h) consisting of a combination of learning theory, group discussion, roleplay and other exercises
  • 1-on-1 coaching practice with your peers and some of our facilitators
  • Independent study/reflection/homework: Min 2h/week
  • A lot of fun and cool new friends!

Post-Training Time Commitment

  • 16 week blocks (each group lasts 16 weeks)
  • 2h / week for each group
  • 1h / week for each 1-on-1 client
  • 2h workshop each week (optional)
  • 2h case review each week (optional)

Facilitator Responsibilities

  • Facilitate peer support groups either by yourself or with a co-facilitator and/or offer 1-on-1 coaching
  • Proactively reach out for support with any issues you encounter in your work with clients
  • Complete post-session documentation
  • Work in alignment with our Ethical Framework

Requirements

  • You want to give back to the community by becoming a Quest of Life facilitator
  • You've completed at least one of our groups. 16 week groups are preferable, but we will accept applicants who've only completed a 4 week group.
  • You're curious and eager to learn more about coaching and group facilitation
  • You're ready to go out your comfort zone and want to be challenged during training
  • You're aligned with Quest of Life's values and ethical framework
  • You're a caring person and a curious and empathetic listener
  • You're consistent, and able to work independently - managing your own schedule and making sure to fulfil your responsibilities with minimal oversight
  • You're driven to give back to the community and want to create a positive impact
  • You're a strong communicator in both written and verbal form
  • You're able to stay remain calm in any situation
  • You're friendly and approachable - easy to talk to and easy to trust and open up to
  • You value difference and are highly accepting of individuals from all backgrounds
  • You already have a strong support system

Benefits (training)

  • Free coaching/facilitation training developed and run by current Quest of Life facilitators
  • On successful completion of training, you may be invited to become a Quest of Life facilitator
  • On completion of training, you will get access to our social server for Quest of Life volunteers past and present

Benefits (for active facilitators)

  • Join a community of passionate and caring individuals
  • Access to ongoing learning opportunities such as workshops in our facilitator server
  • Access to coaching and facilitation related support in group Case Review sessions and during experienced facilitators' Office Hours
  • Access to general 1-1 support for anything work or life related
  • Opportunities to contribute towards shaping the direction of the organisation
